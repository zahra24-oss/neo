Na Jaemin, member of NCT Dream, will soon complete his photo exhibition entitled "NARCISSISM - JAEMIN 1st EXHIBITION". The exhibition was a great success and received much praise from fans due to its thorough preparation and dedication.

The exhibition will be held for 3 weeks, from 29 June - 19 July 2024 at the Hannam Center of Contemporary Cultural Studies (CCCS) in Seoul, South Korea.
